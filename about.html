What We Do

Jemi Neil Consulting (JNC) is a people and business solutions company. We are an organization with two core diverse areas: International Education Consultancy (University & College Placements) & Human Resources Consultancy focusing on executing various Human Resource (HR) strategic initiatives in order for our clients to maximize its best in people. We offer end to end HR solution services ranging from HR Business Partnering, Talent Sourcing & Recruitment, Training & Development, Outsourcing, Employee Engagement, Payroll Management & Administration through to Performance Management. Our team of experienced Human Resources professionals and Educational Counsellors represent our passion for providing top nutch solutions and service delivery techniques. Jemi Neil Consulting (JNC) was founded by skilled professionals with Human Resources experiences, qualifications and professional backgrounds, who identified the existing gap in Talent Solution Services particuarly in the SME space. Thus, JNC was founded to bridge the gap of providing “one stop shop” talent solution services. We observed that, despite the large number of HR Consultancy Agencies, there is still a huge gap in overral Talent Solution Services. We are also able to link our strong Educational consultancy expertise in alignment with HR Solutions. We have provided career development counselling to students whom we eventually placed in jobs on completion of their University courses and in return eventually became our clients. Founded on five (5) core values of Timely Delivery, Integrity, Ownership, Can-do Spirit and Professionalism. The goal of JNC is “to be a leading Global Human Resource Solutions company blending continuous excellent Education advisory services”. We do not intend to just “talk the talk”, we will “walk the talk” as we aim to continually build and develop capacity to deliver world class services.

Mission On Sight

“To provide “one stop shop” Human Resource solution services that will close skill gap to individuals and corporate organizations in todays environment where talent is scarce”. “To provide result-oriented guidance in assisting prospective international students make the best career decisions in their overall course of study and choice institution”.
