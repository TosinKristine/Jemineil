Professional Talent Sourcing & Recruitment

Every organization, whether Small, medium enterprise (SMES) to multinationals has established a paradigm shift in talent sourcing and recruitment. They are no longer focusing on recruiting just any pool of employees but talent to match their organization culture, structure, industry experience, person specification and competency requirements. In the words of successful and celebrated American oil well firefighter- Red Adair “If you think it's expensive to hire a professional to do the job, wait until you hire an amateur”. At Jemi Neil Consulting, we pride ourselves to have the very specific competence, knowledge, experience, large network and accomplished sourcing skills to identify, assess and finally recruit talent across industries. We do not stop there, we proceed to ensure that due backgound and reference checks have been condudcted on all candidates sourced by us. We have a rich pool of registered and qualified candidates both locally and internationally on our database. If you think a role is dificult to source with the right talent, try us.

Executive Search

We understand that headhunting executives are extremely critical to any business. As such, our executive recruitment process is approached differently. We continuously network and engage heavily with the C-level target audience to keep abreast with the most talented and experienced pool of executives across industries.

Outsourcing

Organizations may choose to outsource their entire HR department to us as external Business Partners who will be committed and responsible for the end to end HR Management of its team. We understand how critical an HR team is to any organization and have the capacity to close this gap. We may sometimes second one of our seasoned and experienced HR Business Partners to ensure smooth internal day to day running of the unit. We also understand that HR Managers and Business Managers often prefer to focus less of daily employee services and administration challenges. Hence, we second qualified and professional outsourced staff/manpower across all industries to our clients based on defined short term project or contract duration and/or on long term agreements.

Training & Development

We understand that people have different learning styles. At Jemi Neil Consulting, we ensure all training are in alignment with best practise training cycle to meet the needs of every individual. We offer instructor-led training, and other learning and development opportunities through our various reputable technical partners. We will evaluate your needs and then recommend the ideal training modules that will 'best' develop your staff.

Employee Engagement

At Jemi Neil Consulting, we understand that employee engagement is key in talent management and retention for organizations. We support our clients in articulating and coordinating workplace employee engagement initiatives to foster team building, change management and culture evaluation. We believe that employee engagement begins with the personal engagement of the business owners and internal HR team. We offer products to help leaders, managers and supervisors manage their values, behaviors, and working style preferences. We have expertise in a number of areas, including:

  • Building skills for employee collaboration and improving internal capacity to align business and employee goals.
  • Assisting organizations in the designing & implementation of employee engagement activities.
  • Communicating and sharing results and impact of employee engagement activities with key audiences.

Performance Management

Organizations are moving beyond measuring and rewarding 'activity' to being more 'result oriented'. We understand that the only way to measure effective individual employee and overrall organization performance is through effective 'performance management'. We can help our clients put in place realistic and measurable performance management structures which ensures delivery in result or areas requiring performance improvement.
